About CityConnect: Connecting Citizens and Authorities

CityConnect is more than just an app‚Äîit's the direct line between you and a better city.

We believe that local government works best when it can hear the voices of the people it serves. Our platform is designed to break down bureaucratic barriers by providing a single, simple place for citizens to report non-emergency issues like potholes, street waste, broken lights, and graffiti.

How We Work

  • For the Citizen: You quickly log an issue with precise location and a photo. No more long phone calls, confusing forms, or searching for the right department.
  • For the Authorities: CityConnect instantly routes your report to the correct municipal department or government agency responsible for fixing that specific problem. This ensures quick acknowledgment and efficient delegation of tasks.

By streamlining the process, CityConnect ensures that your report goes to the right person, right away, drastically reducing response times and improving the overall quality of life in your neighborhood. We turn good intentions into effective action.
